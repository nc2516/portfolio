Overview

While staying at home due to covid over the summer of 2021, I decided to take SuperHi‚Äôs 8 Week UX Design course. This course project included a design brief and design guideline to follow. The goal of this project is to create a responsive e-commerce platform, called Marketplace All, that allows users to discover items and checkout as seamlessly as possible, utilizing the UX Design process.
Problem Statement:
Large companies dominate the furniture and home goods scene with mass produced, low quality, plain, and unsustainable items. Small brands that make those unique and environmentally friendly home goods are difficult to discover and scattered across the internet.
The primary goal of Marketplace All is to bring different like-minded brands together in one place, so people can discover new things and save time when searching for furniture and objects for their homes.
